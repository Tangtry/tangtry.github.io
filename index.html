    About Me

    Work and Education Experiences

    I am an Assistant Professor in Management Science, School of Management, Xi'an Jiaotong University. Before join XJTU, I obtain my PhD degree from SEM at Tsinghua University. My research interets mainly lie in the area of data driven optimization, integer programming and dynamic machanism design, etc.

    Assistant Professor in Management Science, School of Management, Xi'an Jiaotong University, 2021-now.

    PhD in Management Science and Engineering, School of Economics and Management, Tsinghua University, 2015-2021.

    Visiting Assistant in Research (CSC scholarship) in Operations Management, School of Management, Yale University, 2017-2018.

    B.S. in Logistics Management (minor in Industrial Engineering), School of Economics and Management, Beijing Jiaotong University, 2011-2015.
