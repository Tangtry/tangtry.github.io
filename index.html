About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in Management Science, School of Management at Xi'an Jiaotong University. Before joining XJTU, I obtain my PhD degree from SEM at Tsinghua University. My research interests mainly lie in the area of data driven optimization, game theory and dynamic mechanism design, etc. You can also find my official homepage here.
